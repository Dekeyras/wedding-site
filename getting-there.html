Portlaoise Train Station is located just five minutes' walk from the Hotel. There are regular services from Dublin Hueston Station.

DIRECTIONS BY CAR

Coming from Dublin take the M7 (towards Limerick, Cork). Take exit 16 off the motorway for Portlaoise. Continue to follow signs for Portlaoise. The Church will be on your left, 500 yards after prison.
